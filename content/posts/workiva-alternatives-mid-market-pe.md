# Finding the Right ESG Reporting Partner for Mid-Market PE

While **Workiva** remains a powerful tool for enterprise-level financial and ESG reporting, many mid-market private equity firms find it overly complex and expensive for their specific needs. In 2026, the landscape for ESG software tailored to the mid-market has matured significantly.

Here is a comparison of the top alternatives for mid-market private equity firms looking to streamline their portfolio ESG data collection and reporting.

## Top 4 Alternatives at a Glance

| Feature | Workiva | Novata | Pulsora | Sustain.Life |

## 1. Novata: The Private Equity Specialist
Novata has solidified its position as the go-to alternative for private equity firms. Unlike general reporting tools, Novata was built by PE firms, for PE firms.

*   **Best for:** Firms that need a "guided" experience and a platform that speaks the language of GPs and LPs.
*   **Key Advantage:** Pre-built templates for standard frameworks (SFDR, EDCI) that are easily deployed across portfolio companies.

## 2. Pulsora: The Data Powerhouse
Pulsora (formerly known as a technical leader in the space) offers deep automation capabilities. It is ideal for firms that have complex data sources and need a robust "audit trail" for every metric.

*   **Best for:** Larger mid-market firms with complex global portfolios.
*   **Key Advantage:** Exceptional data integration and "one-click" audit preparedness.

## 3. Sustain.Life: Efficiency for the Mid-Market
If your primary concern is getting your portfolio companies to report basic, high-quality carbon and ESG data without breaking the bank, Sustain.Life is the clear winner.

*   **Best for:** Mid-market firms focusing on carbon footprinting and foundational ESG metrics.
*   **Key Advantage:** Very user-friendly interface for portfolio company managers who are not ESG experts.

## 4. Workiva (The Benchmark)
Workiva is still the gold standard for firms that are planning an IPO or require rigorous SEC-level document linking. However, for most mid-market PE workflows, it is often "too much tool."

---

## Conclusion: Which should you choose?

- **Choose Novata** if you want a platform designed specifically for the Private Equity relationship.
- **Choose Pulsora** if you have complex data and require heavy automation.
- **Choose Sustain.Life** if you need a cost-effective, easy-to-use tool for carbon tracking.
