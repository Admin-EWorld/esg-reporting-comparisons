# Top Software Like Sustain.Life for Non-Financial Reporting

As environmental, social, and governance (ESG) reporting becomes a critical business requirement, platforms like Sustain.Life have emerged as popular solutions for non-financial reporting. However, depending on your organization's size, industry, and specific reporting frameworks, you might be looking for alternatives.

Here are the top alternatives to Sustain.Life for managing non-financial ESG reporting:

## 1. Persefoni
Persefoni is a leading climate management and accounting platform (CMAP). While Sustain.Life offers broad ESG tools, Persefoni specializes deeply in carbon accounting and financial-grade emissions reporting, making it ideal for enterprises and financial institutions needing strict Scope 1, 2, and 3 calculations.

## 2. Workiva
For organizations that need to integrate non-financial reporting directly with their financial filings, Workiva is the gold standard. It provides a unified platform that connects ESG data to SEC and other regulatory reports, offering robust audit trails and collaboration tools that surpass basic carbon accounting software.

## 3. Greenstone
Greenstone provides comprehensive ESG software solutions tailored for mid-to-large enterprises. Unlike some newer platforms, Greenstone offers deep supply chain sustainability tracking and supports multiple global frameworks (GRI, SASB, TCFD), making it a robust alternative for comprehensive non-financial disclosure.

## 4. Novata
If your focus is on private markets, Novata is a strong contender. Built specifically for private equity firms and their portfolio companies, Novata simplifies the collection of non-financial data, offering industry-specific benchmarks that generalist tools like Sustain.Life might lack.

## Conclusion
Choosing the right ESG software depends on your core needs: carbon accounting precision (Persefoni), integrated financial reporting (Workiva), supply chain depth (Greenstone), or private market focus (Novata). Evaluate your primary frameworks and stakeholder requirements before making a transition.
