For real estate portfolio managers, general-purpose ESG tools often fall short of the specialized data needs of the built environment. While **Sustain.Life** is a strong entry-point for many SMBs, real estate professionals typically require deeper integration with building systems, utility meters, and industry-standard benchmarks like GRESB.

In this guide, we compare the top 5 alternatives to Sustain.Life specifically tailored for real estate asset management.

## 1. Measurabl: The Industry Standard
Measurabl is widely considered the leading ESG platform for real estate. It automates the collection of energy, water, and waste data directly from utility providers and integrates it into audit-ready reports.

*   **Best for:** Large commercial portfolios and those reporting to GRESB.
*   **Key Advantage:** Robust automation and a massive database for benchmarking performance against peers.
*   **Alternative to Sustain.Life because:** It offers deeper real-estate-specific functionality like automated utility sync.

## 2. Deepki: AI-Driven Intelligence
Deepki uses customer data and its own proprietary database of over 1.5 million buildings to provide actionable insights. It’s particularly strong in the European market, helping managers navigate complex regulations like CSRD and SFDR.

*   **Best for:** European asset managers and those looking for AI-driven "next steps" to improve performance.
*   **Key Advantage:** Strong focus on the entire building lifecycle, from acquisition to disposal.
*   **Alternative to Sustain.Life because:** It provides predictive modeling specifically for building efficiency.

## 3. Facilio: Operational-First ESG
Facilio bridges the gap between building operations and ESG reporting. Unlike "point solutions" that only track emissions, Facilio integrates directly with IoT sensors and HVAC systems to turn raw operational data into real-time insights.

*   **Best for:** Facilities teams and managers who want to improve building performance, not just report on it.
*   **Key Advantage:** Real-time monitoring and direct integration with building management systems (BMS).
*   **Alternative to Sustain.Life because:** It moves beyond "lagging indicators" into real-time operational sustainability.

## 4. EnergyCAP: Utility Management Powerhouse
EnergyCAP focuses heavily on the "E" in ESG—specifically energy management. It excels at complex bill processing and auditing, ensuring that the foundational data for your carbon footprint is accurate and verified.

*   **Best for:** Portfolios with complex utility billing and a need for high-precision energy auditing.
*   **Key Advantage:** Advanced utility bill tracking and direct integration with ENERGY STAR® Portfolio Manager®.
*   **Alternative to Sustain.Life because:** It offers more granular utility auditing and cost-tracking features.

## 5. Singu FM: Digitalizing Building Operations
Singu FM is a CAFM (Computer-Aided Facility Management) platform that has evolved to include strong ESG modules. It focuses on the day-to-day operations of a building, ensuring that sustainability is baked into regular maintenance and inspections.

*   **Best for:** Property managers who want a single tool for both operations and sustainability reporting.
*   **Key Advantage:** Integrated mobile app for on-site inspections and IoT data aggregation.
*   **Alternative to Sustain.Life because:** It combines facility management with ESG data collection in one interface.

### Conclusion
If you are a real estate portfolio manager outgrowing Sustain.Life, your next move depends on your primary pain point. For **benchmarking and GRESB**, Measurabl is the go-to. For **European compliance**, look at Deepki. For **operational efficiency**, Facilio or Singu FM offer the best real-time data.
