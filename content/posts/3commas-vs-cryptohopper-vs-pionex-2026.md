As the crypto market evolves in 2026, automated trading has shifted from a luxury to a necessity. For traders looking to leverage AI and algorithmic strategies, three names dominate the conversation: **3Commas**, **Cryptohopper**, and **Pionex**.

In this guide, we break down their features, pricing, and which one you should choose for your specific trading style.

## 1. 3Commas: The Professional Choice
3Commas is the go-to for serious traders who want a terminal that connects to multiple exchanges. It is famous for its **SmartTrade** terminal and highly customizable **DCA and Grid bots**.

*   **Best For:** Advanced traders who need deep customization.
*   **Key Advantage:** Exceptional multi-pair DCA bots and pre-set profitable strategies.
*   **Pricing:** Free basic plan; paid plans from $22 to $99/month.
*   **Recommendation:** [Get Started with 3Commas here](https://3commas.io/?utm_source=referral&utm_medium=cabinet&c=tc2188003)

## 2. Cryptohopper: The "Always On" Cloud Bot
Cryptohopper is a cloud-based trading bot, meaning it runs 24/7 without needing your computer to stay on. It features a unique **Strategy Designer** and a Marketplace where you can buy signals from world-class traders.

*   **Best For:** Traders who want to "Mirror Trade" or follow expert signals.
*   **Key Advantage:** 100% cloud-based with an excellent Marketplace for templates and signals.
*   **Pricing:** $19 to $99/month.
*   **Recommendation:** [Get Started with Cryptohopper here](https://www.cryptohopper.com/?atid=40331)

## 3. Pionex: The Best Free Built-in Bots
Unlike the others, Pionex is actually an **exchange** that comes with 16 free trading bots built directly into the platform. You don't need to connect an API key; you just deposit and start a bot.

*   **Best For:** Beginners and those who want to avoid monthly subscription fees.
*   **Key Advantage:** Zero monthly fees for using the bots; you only pay a low 0.05% trading fee.
*   **Pricing:** FREE (Bot use).
*   **Recommendation:** [Join Pionex and Start Your Free Bot](https://www.pionex.com/en/signUp?r=0GXrQneqfgJ)
