# Best GRESB Reporting Software for Real Estate Funds in 2026

When it comes to real estate and infrastructure, the **Global Real Estate Sustainability Benchmark (GRESB)** is the gold standard for Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) reporting. However, gathering the massive amount of property-level data, validating it, and aligning it with GRESB’s rigorous assessment requirements can be overwhelming.

For real estate funds, joint ventures, and asset managers, utilizing specialized ESG reporting software is no longer optional—it's essential. In this comparison, we explore the top software platforms designed specifically to streamline GRESB reporting, improve scores, and enhance asset value.

## 1. Measurabl
**Best For:** Overall GRESB Reporting and Broad Market Adoption

Measurabl is arguably the most widely adopted ESG data management platform for commercial real estate. According to the company, over 200 funds trust Measurabl for their ESG reporting needs, often resulting in significant GRESB score improvements.

**Key Features:**
- **Automated Data Capture:** Syncs directly with utility providers to automate energy, water, and waste data collection.
- **GRESB Integration:** Features dedicated GRESB export capabilities and aligns perfectly with the GRESB assessment timeline.
- **Portfolio Visibility:** Offers high-level portfolio views as well as granular, property-level analytics.

**Pros:** Unmatched market presence; highly streamlined for the GRESB timeline.  
**Cons:** Can be expensive for smaller portfolios.

## 2. Scaler Global
**Best For:** Actionable Insights and Asset Value Enhancement

Scaler is a rapidly growing platform that focuses heavily on converting fragmented sustainability data into actionable, real-time insights that safeguard asset value and boost Net Operating Income (NOI).

**Key Features:**
- **Decarbonization Roadmaps:** Helps identify underperforming properties and tracks assets against CRREM pathways.
- **Streamlined Validation:** Automates data validation for audit-ready reporting to GRESB, SFDR, and CSRD.
- **Retrofit Optimization:** Provides scenario modeling for capital expenditure on sustainable retrofits.

**Pros:** Excellent for modeling retrofits and tracking against specific decarbonization pathways like CRREM.  
**Cons:** Newer to the market compared to established giants like Measurabl.

## 3. UL Solutions (ESG Advisory & Software)
**Best For:** Expert-Led Guidance and Complex Portfolios

UL Solutions provides a combination of robust software tools and expert advisory services. Their Turbo Buildings software, combined with their advisory arm, is ideal for funds that need both technological infrastructure and strategic guidance.

**Key Features:**
- **Data Support:** Strong capabilities in developing and assessing renewable energy projects.
- **Advisory Services:** Access to expert-led guidance for pre-submission checks and strategic performance improvements.
- **Custom Integrations:** Highly customizable for complex, multinational portfolios.

**Pros:** Backed by UL's massive global presence and scientific expertise; great for companies needing both software and consulting.  
**Cons:** The platform can feel heavier and less "plug-and-play" than pure SaaS solutions.

## 4. Deepki
**Best For:** European Markets and Deep Asset Intelligence

Originally founded in France, Deepki has become a powerhouse in European real estate ESG, though it is expanding globally. It focuses on using data intelligence to drive the transition to net zero.

**Key Features:**
- **Deepki Ready:** Their SaaS platform collects and aggregates data from hundreds of sources globally.
- **Regulatory Alignment:** Extremely strong on European regulations (SFDR, EU Taxonomy) alongside GRESB.
- **Action Plans:** Translates data into concrete ESG action plans at the asset level.

**Pros:** Exceptional understanding of the complex European regulatory landscape.  
**Cons:** Global support outside of Europe is still catching up to their EU dominance.

## Summary

Choosing the right GRESB reporting software depends heavily on your fund's size, geographic focus, and internal expertise. 

- If you want the **industry standard** with automated utility syncing, **Measurabl** is the safest bet.
- If you are focused on **CRREM pathways** and detailed decarbonization modeling, **Scaler Global** is an excellent choice.
- For **complex global portfolios** needing advisory support, look to **UL Solutions**.
- If your assets are primarily in **Europe**, **Deepki** offers unmatched regional regulatory alignment.
