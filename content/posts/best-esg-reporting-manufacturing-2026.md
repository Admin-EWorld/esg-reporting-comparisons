For publicly traded manufacturing companies in 2026, the complexity of ESG reporting has reached a new peak. With CSRD in full swing and the SEC's climate disclosure rules solidified, the choice of software is critical for maintaining investor trust and regulatory compliance.

### Top ESG Software Picks for Manufacturing

#### 1. Workiva
**Best for: Financial Integration & Audit-Ready Disclosures**
Workiva remains the gold standard for public companies. Its platform allows for seamless integration between financial reporting and ESG disclosures. For manufacturers, its ability to connect disparate data sources from various facilities into a single "source of truth" is invaluable for auditability.

#### 2. Sphera
**Best for: Complex Global Operations & Industrial Compliance**
Sphera is highly specialized for the industrial sector. It excels in EHS (Environment, Health, and Safety) integration, making it a natural fit for manufacturers with complex supply chains and heavy environmental footprints.
