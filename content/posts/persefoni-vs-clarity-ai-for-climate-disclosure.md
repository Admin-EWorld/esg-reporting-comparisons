# Persefoni vs Clarity AI for Climate Disclosure

When choosing a climate disclosure and carbon accounting platform, **Persefoni** and **Clarity AI** are two of the leading solutions for enterprise ESG reporting.

## Persefoni Overview
Persefoni is a dedicated carbon accounting and climate management platform built for financial institutions and large enterprises. It excels at Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions tracking, providing deep integration with global frameworks like TCFD, SEC rules, and PCAF (Partnership for Carbon Accounting Financials).

**Pros:**
- Exceptional financial services and PCAF support.
- Audit-ready reporting with robust data lineage.
- SEC disclosure rule compliance out of the box.

## Clarity AI Overview
Clarity AI is a comprehensive sustainability tech platform that uses machine learning and big data to deliver environmental and social insights. While it offers carbon accounting, its broader strength lies in ESG risk analysis, impact investing metrics, and regulatory alignment (e.g., SFDR, EU Taxonomy).

**Pros:**
- Massive database of ESG metrics for public and private companies.
- Advanced AI for data gap filling and portfolio analysis.
- Covers a wider range of ESG topics beyond just carbon.

## The Verdict
If your primary need is strictly **audit-grade carbon accounting** and climate disclosure readiness (especially for financial portfolios), **Persefoni** is the superior choice. However, if you require a broader ESG analytics platform for **impact investing, EU regulatory alignment**, and general sustainability metrics across your portfolio, **Clarity AI** provides a more comprehensive suite.
