# Budget-Friendly ESG Software for CSDDD Preparation

The Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive (CSDDD) requires companies to identify and mitigate environmental and human rights impacts across their value chains. For many companies, finding an affordable solution is key. Here are some budget-friendly ESG software vendors for CSDDD preparation:

1. **Sustain.Life**: Offers an accessible platform for companies to measure, manage, and report their environmental impact, with features tailored for supply chain due diligence and CSDDD compliance.

2. **Greenstone**: Provides flexible and scalable ESG software solutions. Their supply chain module is robust for tracking supplier compliance without breaking the bank.

3. **EcoVadis**: While primarily a ratings platform, their basic tiers offer a cost-effective way for companies to start assessing their suppliers' sustainability performance in line with CSDDD requirements.

4. **KpiESG**: A modular ESG platform that allows companies to select only the features they need, making it a budget-friendly option for early-stage CSDDD reporting and risk assessment.

By leveraging these budget-friendly tools, companies can effectively prepare for CSDDD compliance and ensure sustainable supply chain practices.
