# Alternative to Manual GRI Reporting for Mid-Market Firms

Global Reporting Initiative (GRI) standards provide a comprehensive framework for sustainability reporting. However, manual reporting using spreadsheets is error-prone, time-consuming, and difficult to audit. Mid-market firms often struggle with the resource demands of manual reporting.

## Top Alternatives to Manual Reporting

### 1. Workiva
Workiva offers a robust platform for ESG reporting, integrating seamlessly with existing financial reporting tools. It provides extensive automation capabilities, reducing the risk of errors and improving data accuracy.

### 2. Sustain.Life
Sustain.Life is a cost-effective solution tailored for mid-market firms. It simplifies the carbon accounting process and provides templates aligned with GRI standards, making it easier to generate compliant reports without extensive manual effort.

### 3. Persefoni
While known for carbon accounting, Persefoni also supports broader ESG reporting frameworks including GRI. Its AI-driven insights and automated data ingestion can significantly streamline the reporting process.

### 4. Clarity AI
Clarity AI leverages machine learning to automate data collection and analysis, providing mid-market firms with actionable insights and simplifying the path to GRI compliance.

### Conclusion
Transitioning from manual GRI reporting to automated software solutions can save time, reduce errors, and improve the overall quality of sustainability disclosures. Workiva, Sustain.Life, Persefoni, and Clarity AI are excellent alternatives for mid-market firms seeking to streamline their ESG reporting.
