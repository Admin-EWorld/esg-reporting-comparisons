# Best ESG Software for Asset Management Compliance

In the highly regulated world of asset management, compliance with Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) standards is no longer optional. Asset managers need robust software to aggregate data, ensure regulatory compliance (such as SFDR in Europe), and report accurately to stakeholders.

Here are the top 3 ESG software solutions tailored for asset management compliance:

## 1. Clarity AI
Clarity AI uses machine learning to provide sustainability insights across millions of companies and funds. It integrates directly into existing wealth management platforms and portfolio management systems, making it an excellent choice for seamless SFDR and EU Taxonomy reporting.

## 2. MSCI ESG Research
A heavy hitter in the financial industry, MSCI provides comprehensive ESG ratings and data. Their tools are deeply integrated with global benchmarks and allow asset managers to conduct thorough portfolio screening and stress-testing against climate scenarios.

## 3. Sustainalytics (Morningstar)
Sustainalytics offers extensive corporate ESG ratings and research. Their platform is highly customizable, allowing asset managers to assess material ESG risks at the portfolio level and generate compliance-ready reports for regulatory bodies.

## Conclusion
Choosing the right ESG software depends on your firm's specific regulatory exposure and existing tech stack. Clarity AI shines in AI-driven integration, MSCI is unparalleled for benchmark comparisons, and Sustainalytics offers deep, customizable risk research.
