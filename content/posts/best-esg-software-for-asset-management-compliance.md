# Best ESG Software for Asset Management Compliance

Asset managers face increasing pressure to comply with ESG regulations like SFDR and TCFD. Here are the top software solutions for asset management compliance:

## 1. Clarity AI
Clarity AI offers a comprehensive platform designed specifically for asset managers to align their portfolios with sustainability goals and regulatory requirements. It provides robust data coverage and intuitive reporting tools.

## 2. Workiva
Workiva is an industry leader in compliance reporting. Its platform connects ESG data with financial reporting, ensuring audit-ready disclosures that meet the stringent demands of asset management compliance.

## 3. Persefoni
Specializing in carbon accounting, Persefoni is ideal for asset managers focused on climate-related disclosures and financed emissions calculations, ensuring compliance with PCAF and TCFD standards.

## 4. MSCI ESG Manager
MSCI provides extensive ESG ratings and analytics, enabling asset managers to seamlessly integrate ESG factors into their investment strategies and compliance reporting processes.

## Conclusion
Choosing the right ESG software depends on your specific compliance needs, whether it's comprehensive sustainability reporting like Clarity AI or specialized carbon accounting like Persefoni.
