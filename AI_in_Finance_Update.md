## Research Summary: AI in Finance

Artificial Intelligence (AI) in finance involves using advanced technology, algorithms, and Machine Learning (ML) to analyze data, automate tasks, and enhance decision-making across financial services.

### Key Applications and Benefits:
*   Driving insights for data analytics, performance measurement, predictions, and forecasting.
*   Enabling real-time calculations and intelligent data retrieval.
*   Playing a significant role in automated and high-speed trading in liquid markets.
*   Augmenting human intelligence through pattern recognition and learning from data.
